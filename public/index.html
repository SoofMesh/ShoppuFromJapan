Shipping

DO YOU SHIP WORLDWIDE?

Yes, we ship to countries on Japan’s Post’ list. Please contact us or visit their website (Japanese Only) for more details.

WHAT IS PRE-APPROVED SHIPPING?

With pre-approved shipping, you are approving the shipping costs beforehand. We will order the items right away for you in and issue a refund for the remaining shipping amount. If the shipping is more than the pre-approved amount (Yen 2,500), we will contact you first to pay the reamaining amount and then continue to order your items.

I LOST MY TRACKING NUMBER CAN YOU RECOVER IT?

Please contact us and we will try and recover your tracking number.



Refunds and Cancellations

WHAT IS YOUR REFUND POLICY?

100% Refund back if we have not started with your order yet or your items are not available anymore. No refunds possible in case we have started and processed your order.

I WANT TO CANCEL MY ORDER.

You can only cancel your order if we have not received your payment yet. After you have paid, we can only cancel in case we have not ordered your items yet. Please contact us through our live chat or contact form.

WHAT IF THE ITEM I WANT IS OUT OF STOCK?

100% Refund in case your item is out of stock. However, we will try our best to find the same item on another site or store on your behalf.

HOW LONG DOES A REFUND TAKE?

While we try to refund you within 48-hours, refunds might take up to 3 business days.

CAN I PAY IN A DIFFERENT CURRENCY?

Only Japanese Yen is accepted.



Payment & Item Storage

DO YOU ACCEPT OTHER PAYMENTS THAN PAYPAL?

At the moment we only accept payments through PayPal.

CAN YOU HOLD ON TO MY ITEM FOR A WHILE

Yes we can hold your item for 4-weeks. Need to store it longer? Please contact us and we will see what we can do for you.



Auction Sites and Second Hand Items

ABOUT AUCTION AND FLEA MARKET SITES.

Items bought through Auction and Flea market sites can not be returned whatsoever. While usually there is not a problem, in case there is, we are not responsible for any damages, misleading pictures or refunds.

ABOUT FAKE ITEMS.

Shoppu from Japan is not responsible for any fake items or the refund for it. We do not return or refund fake items and do not have the authority to decide if the item is authentic.
